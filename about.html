Critter Gitters Pro is a home grown business based out of Wichita KS. We provide quality nuisance animal removal and restoration services for a fair price in the Great Bend, Wichita, and Hays areas. The techniques and skills used by our professionals have been passed down for five generations. Our knowledge base helps us to remove animals effectively, timely, and humanely. We have removed coyotes, raccoons, squirrels, skunks, bees, wasps, gophers, badgers, beavers, bats, cats, bobcats, and rats. We believe that although these animals are nuisances and sometime frightening, they serve their purposes to the environment. That is why we safely reintegrate the animals back into the wild whenever possible. We also remove baby animals and animal carcasses. We strive for great customer service at fair prices. If you have any questions please feel free to call or message us for a complimentary estimate.
